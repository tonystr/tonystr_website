# Everything is a link

[Can I write](https://www.orwellfoundation.com/the-orwell-foundation/orwell/essays-and-other-works/why-i-write/) [a blog post](https://themeisle.com/blog/history-of-blogging/) [such that](https://www.mathsisfun.com/sets/set-builder-notation.html#:~:text=such%20that) [all text](https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode17.0.0/) [is a link](https://html.spec.whatwg.org/dev/links.html#links)? [Punctuation doesn't count](https://style.mla.org/punctuation-following-linked-text/), [so I can substitute](https://grammarstreet.com/2025/04/30/the-power-of-substitution-in-english-grammar-a-complete-guide/) ["and" for ampersand (&)](https://grammarhow.com/vs-and/). [I have banned](https://www.law.georgetown.edu/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/Stacie-Smith-Effective-Rules-Handout.pdf) [wikipedia links](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_Wikipedia) & [self-references](https://www.imm.dtu.dk/~tobo/essay.pdf), [except](https://linguisticreflections.wordpress.com/2025/02/11/reflections-on-exceptions-and-suppletions/) [in this sentence](http://tonystr.net/blog/everything_is_a_link#:~:text=I%20have%20banned%20wikipedia%20links%20and%20self%2Dreferences%2C%20except%20for%20in%20this%20sentence). [Repeat links are also banned](https://www.aconsciousrethink.com/19891/i-hate-repeating-myself/). [Underline links](https://webaim.org/techniques/hypertext/link_text#underlining) [have been enabled](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/Reference/Properties/text-decoration) [only for this page](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/4597050/how-to-check-if-the-url-contains-a-given-string), [otherwise it would be impossible](https://lawsofux.com/law-of-similarity/) [to tell the links apart](https://www.4menearme.com/tools/compare-two-urls/).

[It is only natural](https://www.gutenberg.org/cache/epub/2680/pg2680-images.html#:~:text=but%20as%20for%20honour%20and%20praise%2C%20that%20they%20ought%20not%20generally%20to%20be%20admitted%20and%20accepted%20of%20from%20all%2C%20but%20from%20such%20only%2C%20who%20live%20according%20to%20nature) [to discuss](https://www.productlogz.com/blog/why-are-forums-important-the-benefits-of-online-discussion-forums) [hypertext references](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/225070375_The_Semantic_Web_A_New_Form_of_Web_Content_That_is_Meaningful_to_Computers_Will_Unleash_a_Revolution_of_New_Possibilities) [in a post like this](https://poets.org/text/brief-guide-oulipo). [Links were originally](https://www.wired.com/story/google-link-hyperlink-seo/) [intended to connect websites](https://www.w3.org/History/1989/proposal.html), [but the lack of backlinks](https://blog.aspiration.marketing/en/the-missing-link-in-seo-why-you-lose-backlinks-and-what-to-do) [make most links a one-way reference](https://www.rhinorank.io/blog/what-are-one-way-links/). [Webrings](https://petrapixel.neocities.org/coding/webring-list) & [the indie web](https://theindieweb.com/) [try](https://webring.xxiivv.com/) [their](https://kagi.com/smallweb/) [best](https://512kb.club/) [to link sites together to satisfy](https://smallweb.cc/?r=5018) [the dream of an interconnected web](https://www.dbreunig.com/2025/06/16/drawbridges-go-up.html). [Most](https://apnews.com/article/severe-weather-anxiety-mental-health-08e3e5d1817512d6eaff6393f165ea6e) [large](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/04/nx-s1-5830291/2026-hurricane-season-cell-phones) [websites](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/05/protecting-players-and-fans-during-fifa-world-cup-2026/) [today simply use links as a](https://www.annehelmond.nl/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/Helmond-2018-A-historiography-of-the-hyperlink.pdf) [low-effort source-attribution mechanism](https://www.plagiarismtoday.com/2023/04/04/the-power-of-links-for-citation/). [Unfortunately, link decay](https://macwright.com/2020/05/02/linkrot) [may one day lead to a dead internet](https://thedeadinternettheory.com/). [Internet archives](https://archive.org/) [are vital for preserving](https://www.encyclopedia.com/media/encyclopedias-almanacs-transcripts-and-maps/preservation-and-conservation-information) [this information](https://explodingtopics.com/blog/data-generated-per-day) [in the long term](https://archiveprogram.github.com/arctic-vault/).
